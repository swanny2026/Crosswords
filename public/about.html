About Crosswords

Crosswords is a free daily word puzzle game designed to be simple to pick up, satisfying to solve, and impossible to put down. Every day brings a fresh challenge — reveal the hidden words in a crossword grid by guessing one letter at a time.

Unlike traditional crosswords that rely on cryptic clues, Crosswords works differently. You're shown an empty grid, and your task is to uncover the words by guessing which letters appear. Guess a correct letter and every matching cell lights up at once. Guess wrong and you lose one of your limited tries. It's part word game, part deduction puzzle, and entirely addictive.

Who it's for

Crosswords was built for anyone who loves words and enjoys a daily mental workout. Whether you're a lifelong crossword enthusiast or someone looking for a gentle, rewarding way to keep your mind sharp, the game is welcoming and easy to learn. There are no complicated rules and no pressure — just you, the grid, and the quiet satisfaction of cracking each puzzle.

Many of our players enjoy it as part of their morning routine, alongside a cup of tea. It's particularly popular with people who grew up doing newspaper crosswords and want something familiar but fresh on their phone or tablet.

What makes it special

  • A new challenge every day — come back daily to keep your streak alive.
  • 500 levels to work through — plenty to keep you busy at your own pace.
  • Compete with friends — climb the leaderboard with your daily streak, level progress, and fastest times.
  • Completely free — no charges, no paywalls.
  • No download needed — play instantly in your browser, or add it to your home screen like an app.

A daily habit worth keeping. Solving a word puzzle each day is a lovely way to start the morning — a small, achievable win that gets the mind ticking over. Build a streak, challenge your friends, and enjoy a fresh grid every single day.

How it started

Crosswords began as a passion project — a simple idea to create the kind of daily word game that families could share and enjoy together. It grew through word of mouth, with players sharing it with friends and family who were looking for a fun, friendly puzzle to play each day. That community spirit is still at the heart of the game today.
